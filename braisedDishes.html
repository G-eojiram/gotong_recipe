How to Cook Braised Dishes?

What is Braised Dishes?

 In Filipino cuisine, a braised dish is commonly referred to as "adobo." Adobo is a cooking method where meat, often chicken or pork, is marinated in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, andvarious spices. The marinated meat is then simmered until tender, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to reduce and thicken. The result is a savory and flavorful dish with a rich sauce.

Adobo is a staple in Filipino households, and there are many regional variations of the dish, each with its own unique twist on the basic adobo recipe.

The term "Adobo" itself is used to describe both the cooking method and the resulting dish.
